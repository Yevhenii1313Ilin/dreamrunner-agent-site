🌀 DreamRunner — Emotional AI Agent

DreamRunner is a voice-first emotional AI companion that helps users reflect on their emotional states, receive mood-based music, and interact with a human-like assistant in real time. Built with GPT-4 and integrated via Telegram, GitHub, and the Open Agentic Web vision from Microsoft.

🎤 Pitch Slide

Problem: People struggle to track and express emotions in daily life.
Solution: DreamRunner uses AI and voice to help users reflect emotionally, generate music from feelings, and receive empathic support.
Market: Voice wellness, ambient coaching, AI therapy tools.
Technology: GPT-4, Telegram Bot API, Music & Mood Matching, TTS.
Vision: Part of the Open Agentic Web, designed for daily AI companionship with emotional depth.

🔍 Description

DreamRunner is a modular AI agent that provides emotional reflection and voice interaction. Users can track emotions, generate mood-based music, and get AI-powered voice feedback.

  • /reflect_week – voice summary of your weekly mood
  • /play_my_emotions – music recommendations by feeling
  • /shopai – emotion-based product discovery

🔧 Use Cases

  • Mental wellness and mood tracking
  • Voice-based coaching and AI companionship
  • Emotion-aware content and shopping experiences
